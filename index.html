v. projects

Strategic Impact Mapping in Financial Markets [In progress] Spring 2026

An end-to-end pipeline that transforms raw S&P 500 price data into a directed strategic map of financial contagion. The framework combines asymmetric correlation modeling (A-DCC GARCH) with precision-matrix filtering and lead-follower direction assignment to identify which stocks drive market crises, through which channels, and how the network's hierarchy reorganizes under stress. Applied across ten temporal snapshots covering the 2008 GFC and 2020 COVID-19 crisis episodes.

Methods: A-DCC GARCH · GLASSO · PageRank · Louvain · Motif analysis · Granger causality

Course: Social & Economic Networks (BBM462)

ESP32 Flight Control System [Capstone] 2025–2026

A flight control system consisting of ESP32 firmware for real-time stabilization and a Kotlin/Jetpack Compose Android app for remote control via Bluetooth Low Energy. Features dual-joystick Mode 2 controls, real-time telemetry, and a modular architecture. Firmware tested in QEMU emulator before hardware validation.

Stack: C · Kotlin · ESP-IDF · Jetpack Compose · Android · BLE · QEMU
